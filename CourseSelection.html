Using Optimization to Assign Staff to Courses by Site

This example shows several various approaches depending whether it is important to restrict site (i.e. to reduce cost of running training) and whether courses are mandatory or not.

Let's start by looking at a sample dataset. We have 3 staff, 2 courses and 2 possible sites. Staff have been asked to give preferences on where they do these courses. We've standardised this so that 1 means most preferred and 0 means least or unpreferred.

Let's run our first optimization problem. Here we maximise total preference and use a binary variable to select the combination of <person,course,site> that maximises satisfaction. We add a constraint that for all persons and courses only 1 site is selected. I.e assign staff to the courses in the site they prefer.
